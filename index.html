Platform Capabilities

Every tool your transport operation needs — nothing it doesn't.

Purpose-built for private organisations. Not a generic fleet tracker with your logo on it.

Live GPS Tracking

Driver app sends GPS coordinates every 5–10 seconds via SignalR WebSockets. Updates reach every connected screen — admin dashboard and passenger app — in under 3 seconds. No refresh required.

Dual ETA Intelligence

Two ETA layers shown simultaneously. Scheduled ETA is the static baseline calculated at trip creation. Live ETA recalculates every GPS cycle from the driver's actual position and rolling average speed. Passengers always know both.

Route & Stop Management

Build your route network once. Define stops with GPS coordinates, set their sequence, and assign multiple trips to the same route at different times. Morning and evening runs, weekday-only schedules — all managed from the dashboard.

Driver Management

Invite drivers via email or SMS. Secure token links — no self-registration. Manage driver profiles, assign vehicles, track trip history, and suspend or reinstate access in one click. Drivers join in minutes, not days.

Passenger Management

Three onboarding paths — direct email invite, SMS link, or a shareable public join link with a custom form. Approve or reject requests with a reason. Assign passengers to routes and stops. Full control over who sees what.

GPS Signal Loss Handling

If GPS drops for more than 30 seconds, the system responds automatically — passenger apps show last known location with a signal lost indicator, driver apps trigger an audio alarm, and admin dashboards surface a real-time alert. No manual intervention required.

FAQ

Questions we get before every demo.

Straight answers. No marketing spin.

Will our drivers actually use the app? They're not tech-savvy.

This was our primary design constraint. The driver app has one core screen: a Start Trip button and an End Trip button. That's it. Background GPS runs automatically the moment a trip is started — drivers don't have to think about it. Our target: the core trip action is achievable in one tap from the home screen. Most drivers are operational within 10 minutes of installing the app.

How long does setup actually take?

Most organisations are live within a few days of onboarding. You configure your routes, stops, and vehicles in the admin dashboard — if you already run a structured transport operation, your existing route data maps directly into DWD. Our team is available throughout setup to assist. There's no IT infrastructure to provision and no on-premise software to install.

How accurate is the Live ETA?

Live ETA is calculated from the driver's current GPS position and rolling average speed across the last 5 GPS pings. Our target is ±2 minutes accuracy for 70% of stops. Accuracy depends on GPS signal quality and road conditions — which is exactly why we show the Scheduled ETA simultaneously. Passengers always have a baseline even when live data is imprecise.

What happens when a driver loses GPS signal?

The system handles this automatically. After 30 seconds without a GPS ping, the passenger app switches to the last known location with a "Signal Lost" indicator — Live ETA is replaced by Scheduled ETA. The driver's app triggers an audio and vibration alarm prompting them to check connectivity. Admins see a real-time alert in the dashboard. When signal resumes, live tracking restores without any action from the driver.

We're a small school with only 3 buses. Is DWD right for us?

Yes — the Starter plan is built specifically for smaller operations (up to 5 vehicles, 5 routes, 100 passengers). You get the same live tracking and dual ETA as larger organisations. Many of our early customers are small schools that wanted professional transport visibility without paying enterprise prices or hiring a tech team.

Is our organisation's data kept private?

DWD is a multi-tenant platform with strict isolation enforced at the database level — no org can see another org's data, routes, passengers, or trips. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Invitation tokens are single-use and expire within 24–72 hours. Private trips never appear in public discovery search results unless you explicitly enable it.

How do passengers join and access their trips?

Three ways: you can send a direct email invite, an SMS invite with a secure link, or share a unique public join link for passengers to fill in a request form. Passengers download the DWD passenger app, create an account via their invite link, and immediately see their assigned routes and trips. Unauthenticated users can still search and view discoverable trips without an account.
